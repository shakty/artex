The experiment is divided in two parts: Preparation and Game.

Part 1: Preparation

In this part you will not interact with other players, and you cannot earn points unless explicitly mentioned on the screen. Part 1 involves three steps:
  • First, a basic psychological and demographics survey.
  • Second, preliminary instructions of the game (full details disclosed in Part 2).
  • Third, play in practice mode to get familiar with the game interface.

Part 2: Game

In this part you will interact with other human participants. You might need to wait a short while until enough participants are connected. When enough participants have connected, the full details of the game will be disclosed to you, followed by a brief understanding quiz.
  • The game will be repeated for a certain number of rounds, and in each round you will have a chance to earn points based on your actions and the actions of other players.
  • After the game, a questionnaire consisting in a short compulsory part and in a series of optional questions will follow.
Important! This part of the experiment is synchronous: it requires all participants to be connected and active at the same time. In the eventuality of a disconnection from the server, a popup message will appear with instructions how to reconnect.

Payout scheme

If you complete the whole experiment (Part 1 + Part 2) you will receive rewards points:
  • a fixed reward of 1 points, and
  • a variable bonus depending on your actions in the experiment.
Every X point/s you will earn 1 USD.
